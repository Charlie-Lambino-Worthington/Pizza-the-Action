About us



For several years a popular pizza business operated out of a horsebox in The White Lion car park - and then it closed down, leaving a horsebox but no pizzas. Moving on a couple of years to summer 2023, Gil Lambino and Charlie Worthington aquired a new horsebox, a wood fired pizza oven and in July opened Pizza The Action, just 2 years before we got married The Parish Church OF St. Mary and St. Bartholomewin Hampton-in-Arden.

By using our own sourdough pizza dough and high quality ingredients, our extensive menu caters to all tastes, all made freshly to order. We cater for those with gluten intolerance, celiacs, vegans, and halal. All our pizzas are cooked freshly to order and can be taken away or eaten in the pub or pub garden. To minimise wate and negative effects on the environment, we serve our pizzas on plates if you are eating in, at the pub.

Regular customers are rewarded with a free pizza for every 10th they buy - just ask for a loyalty card.

Opening hours are Thursday, Friday and Saturday evenings from 5.30pm until 9.30pm and Sunday evenings 5.30pm until 8.30pm Orders can be taken at the van or over the telephone at 07835 813 830. Opening hours can vary - for exaple when The White Lion is showing interesting sporting events, Live music or public holidays.

Pizza The Action can be booked for private events such as weddings, parties, charity events or festivals. call 07835 813 830 or email mmm@pizza-the-action.com to discuss your requirements.
